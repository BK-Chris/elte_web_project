Color Scheme

In screen design, designers use the additive color model, where red, green and blue are the primary colors. Just as you need to place images and other elements in visual design strategically, your color choices should optimize your users' experience in attractive interfaces with high usability. When starting your design process, you can consider using any of these main color schemes below.
